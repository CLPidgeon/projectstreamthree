Welcome to the best ice hockey website you ever did see!

This site aims to be the only place you need to go to find information on British ice hockey. There are many ways for you to get involved. Head over to the about page to find out more about us!

You can follow our blog to read about what we're up to this week.

The main event is the fantasy ice hockey league we run each season! Head on over to get yourself involved. Note, this is using EIHL players only - sorry NIHL North & South fans!

We have a forum for you to get involved in. The forum is split into different sections; British ice hockey, World ice hockey and The Bar. You'll need to register for an account to read the forum and contribute your thoughts.

We also have our issue tracker for bugs and features. If you stumble across a bug or want to request a feature just fill in the form! You will need to subscribe to upvote on features though.

Have an issue or something to discuss with us? Head over to the contact page and get in touch.
