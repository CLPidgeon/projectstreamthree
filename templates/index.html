Welcome to the best ice hockey website you ever did see!

This site aims to be the only place you need to go to find information on British ice hockey. There are many ways for you to get involved. Head over to the about page to find out more!

You can follow our blog to read about what we're up to this week.

We have a forum for you to get involved in. The forum is split into different sections; British ice hockey, World ice hockey and The Bar. Go get yourself involved, you'll need to register for an account to contribute though.

We also have our issue tracker for bugs and features. If you find a bug or want to request a feature just fill in the form! You will need to subscribe to upvote on features though.

Finally, you can track how many bugs and features we are dealing with over on our progress page. Here you will find charts showing how many are being dealt with on a daily, weekly and monthly basis.

Have an issue or something to discuss? Head over to the contact page and get in touch.
