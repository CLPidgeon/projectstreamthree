Rules

Fantasy Ice Hockey Rules

Team Set Up:

  1. Teams - You may have up to 3 players from each team.
  2. Netminders - You must choose 2 netminders - 1 British netminder and 1 Import netminder.
  3. Defence - You must choose 6 defencemen - 2 British defencemen and 4 Import defencemen.
  4. Forwards - You must choose 9 forwards - 3 British forwards and 6 Import forwards.

Earning points

Netminders:

  • Shutout - 5 points
  • Saves - 1 point every 10 saves
  • Assist - 3 points
  • Goal - 20 points

Defence

  • Clean sheet - 3 points
  • Assist - 3 points
  • Goal - 5 points

Forwards

  • Assist - 2 points
  • Goal - 3 points

Losing points

All players lose the following points per penalty taken

  • Minor penalty - 1 point
  • Major penalty - 2 points
  • Game penalty - 5 points
  • Match penalty - 10 points
